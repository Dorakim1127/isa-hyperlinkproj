Sweet Porridge

02          Once on a time when the girl had gone out, her mother said, cook, little pot, cook. And it did cook and she ate till she was satisfied, and then she wanted the pot to stop cooking, but did not know the word.

So it went on cooking and the porridge rose

 

 

over

 

 

 

 

the edge,

and still it cooked on

until the kitchen and whole house were full,

and then the next house,

and then the whole street,

just as if it wanted to satisfy

the hunger of the whole world,

and there was the greatest distress,

but no one knew how to stop it.
